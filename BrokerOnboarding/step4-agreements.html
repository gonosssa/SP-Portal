Producer Agreement

ShelterPoint Life Insurance Company Producer Agreement

1. Definitions and Parties

This Agreement is between ShelterPoint Life Insurance Company ("Company") and you, the Producer. The Company authorizes you to solicit and procure applications for insurance programs on a nonterritorial, nonexclusive basis.

2. Relationship

You are an independent contractor, not an employee. Except where state law requires otherwise or you are licensed only as an agent, you are an insurance broker representing your clients.

3. Your Responsibilities

  • Solicit applications and assist applicants in completing them accurately
  • Submit applications and premiums promptly
  • Comply with all underwriting rules and insurance laws
  • Maintain E&O coverage
  • Obtain prior written approval for all marketing materials
  • Keep accurate records

4. Restrictions

You do not have authority to bind the Company, waive policy provisions, alter applications, or offer rebates not specified in the policy.

5. Compensation

Company will pay compensation according to the Compensation Schedule. You must be appropriately licensed to receive compensation. Commission may be offset against amounts you owe the Company.

6. Termination

Either party may terminate this Agreement with 30 days written notice. Agreement terminates automatically upon your death, disability, license revocation, or for dishonesty, fraud, or breach of terms.

7. Indemnification

You agree to indemnify and hold the Company harmless from claims arising from your unlawful or negligent acts, misrepresentations, or breach of this Agreement.

8. Governing Law

This Agreement is governed by the laws of the State of New York.

This is a summary. Full terms are provided in the complete Producer Agreement document.
